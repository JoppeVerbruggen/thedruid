Behind the scenes

The video isn't as good as I wanted it to be. Things didn't really go to plan (like horses going crazy because of the snare drum). Because of Toon not being able to use the snare, he had to improvise a solo. This made filming and syncing a lot harder, because he couldn't play the same thing twice (not exactly).

Because of the not-so-pretty yellow lighting I went with a very little saturated colorgrade.

The audio was recorded on an external recorder, with two extra mics (for extra bass, hihat and snare). I mixed everything in FL Studio (simple EQ and compression) so I didn't have to work with 4 audiotracks in premiere.

Something I wish I did was having one main shot on a tripod, so I always had a backup. This wasn't possible because of the solo being improvisation.
Looking back I could've done better, but I did learn a thing or two because of my mistakes.
