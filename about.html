The Druid

'The Druid' is a composition of 'drum' and 'guide'.

The Druid offers services that help you with everything that has to do with drums, except for playing them. We can help you set up, tune and record your kit. Our technicians bring different cymbal sets and drumheads, to help you choose the sound you're going for. We only work with the best gear, so good sounding drums are guaranteed.
You can either let us assist you, or you can let us worry about the sound, and you just focus on playing the drums.
